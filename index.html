iPhone 2G

Apple reinvents the phone.

  • January 7, 2007
  • iPod + Phone + Internet Communicator
  • Storage Capacity
    • 4, 8, or 16 GB
  • 2MP rear camera
  • Revolution for the smartphone industry
  • Unofficially referred to as iPhone 2G
    • Offically called "iPhone"
  • Carrier support exclusive to AT&T

iPhone 3G

The iPhone you have been waiting for.

  • June 9, 2008
  • Storage Capacity
    • 8 or 16 GB
  • First iPhone with 3G cellular connectivity

iPhone 3GS

The fastest, smartest phone yet.

  • June 8, 2009
  • Storage Capacity
    • 8, 16, or 32 GB
    • First iPhone with storage capacity over 16 GB
  • Improved camera resolution
    • Video recording in 480p
    • 3MP lens
  • First iPhone to record video
  • Added compass feature

iPhone 4

This changes everything. Again.

  • June 7, 2010
  • Storage Capacity
    • 8, 16, or 32 GB
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Video recording in 720p
    • 4MP lens
  • First iPhone with front-facing camera
    • Introduced FaceTime
  • Powered by Apple A4 processor
      • First iPhone with Apple A-series SoC [system-on-a-chip] processor
  • Retina display [940x640]
  • First iPhone to support carriers other than AT&T
  • First iPhone with built-in camera flash
    • Also functioned as a flashlight

iPhone 4s

The most amazing iPhone yet

  • October 4, 2011
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, or 64 GB
      First iPhone with storage capacity over 32 GB
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Video recording in 1080p at 30 fps
    • 8MP lens
    • Face detection
    • Video stabilization
  • Powered by Apple A5 processor
  • Bluetooth 4.0
  • Siri

iPhone 5

The biggest thing to happen to iPhone since iPhone.

  • September 12, 2012
  • Storage Capacity
    • 8, 16, 32, or 64 GB
  • Improved rear camera resolution
  • Powered by Apple A6 processor
  • Screen size increases to 4.0 in.
  • First iPhone with Lightning connector
    • Marked the beginning of the end of the 30-pin connector
  • Marketing slogan changed to "Loving it is easy. That's why so many people do." in March 2013

iPhone 5s

Forward thinking

  • September 10, 2013
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, or 64 GB
  • Added True Tone flash [dual-tone]
  • Powered by Apple A7 processor
  • First iPhone with Touch ID
  • Marketing slogan changed to "You're more powerful than you think." in April 2014

iPhone 5c

For the colorful

  • September 10, 2013
  • Storage Capacity
    • 8, 16, or 32 GB
  • Powered by Apple A6 processor
    • First new iPhone sold without a new chip
  • First iPhone to be sold in more than three colors
  • Built with a full plastic enclosure

iPhone 6

Bigger than bigger

  • September 9, 2014
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, 64, or 128 GB
    • First iPhone with storage capacity over 64 GB
  • Improved rear camera
    • Video recording in 1080p at 60 fps
    • Slo-mo video [120 or 240 fps]
    • Timer mode
  • Improved front-facing camera
    • 720p FaceTime HD camera
    • Burst mode
  • First iPhone with a physical, protruding camera
  • Introduced Apple Pay
  • Powered by Apple A8 processor
  • Screen size increased to 4.7 in.
  • First iPhone with NFC support
  • Marketing slogan later changed to "The two and only"

iPhone 6 Plus

Bigger than bigger

  • September 9, 2014
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 64, or 128 GB
    • First iPhone with storage capacity over 64 GB
    • First iPhone to skip a level of storage capacity
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Video recording in 1080p at 60 fps
    • Slo-mo video [120 or 240 fps]
    • Timer mode
  • Improved front-facing camera resolutioons
    • 720p FaceTime HD camera
    • Burst mode
  • First iPhone with a physical, protruding camera
  • Introduced Apple Pay
  • Powered by Apple A8 processor
  • First iPhone with NFC support
  • Marketing slogan later changed to "The two and only"
  • Identical to iPhone 6 in all aspects except physical size and storage capacity options
    • Screen size increased to 5.5 in.
    • Skipped over 32 GB storage capacity option

iPhone 6s

The only thing that's changed is everything

  • September 9, 2015
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, 64, or 128 GB
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Video recording in 4K at 30 fps
    • 12MP lens
    • Live Photos
  • Powered by Apple A9 processor
  • First iPhone with 3D Touch
  • First iPhone offered in "Rose Gold" finish
  • Marketing slogan later changed to "One powerful phone"

iPhone 6s Plus

slogan

  • September 9, 2015
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, 64, or 128 GB
    • Brought back 32 GB storage capacity option for "Plus" model iPhones
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Video recording in 1080p at 30 fps
    • 12MP lens
    • Live Photos
    • Optical image stalbilization for video
  • Powered by Apple A9 processor
  • First iPhone with 3D Touch
  • First iPhone offered in "Rose Gold" finish
  • Marketing slogan later changed to "One powerful phone"

iPhone SE (1st generation)

A big step for small.

  • March 21, 2016
  • Storage Capacity
    • 16, 32, 64, or 128 GB
  • Powered by Apple A9 processor
  • First iPhone for people who want a physically small phone
    • 4.0 in. screen size
    • Same form-factor as iPhone 5s
  • First "SE" iPhone
    • First budget iPhone
    • Older design, updated internals

iPhone 7

This is 7

  • September 7, 2016
  • Storage Capacity
    • 32, 128, or 256 GB
      • First iPhone with storage capacity over 128 GB
  • Improved rear camera resolution
    • Optical image stabilization
    • Quad-LED True Tone flash
  • Powered by Apple A10 Fusion processor
  • First iPhone with a mid-cycle color refresh
    • First iPhone offered in a (PRODUCT)RED™ finish
  • Only iPhone offered in Jet Black finish
  • First iPhone with a haptic engine
    • Removal of the physical Home Button
  • First iPhone sold without the 3.5mm headphone jack
    • First iPhone to include Lightning EarPods in the box

  • First iPhone to include more than one rear camera

  • Powered by Apple A10 Fusion processor
